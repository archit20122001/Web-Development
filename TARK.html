Tyazhelyi Atomnyi Raketnyi Kreiser(TARK) stands for Heavy Nuclear Missile Cruiser, these types of ships are heavily armed and have a power to take down a whole task force single handedly, they have execellent air defence and they are armed to the teeth. In modern world, there is only one kind of ship class that has this desaignation, the Russian Kirov class is referred as Heavy Nuclear Missile Cruiser because of the firepower they possess.
