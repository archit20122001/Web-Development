RF Shtorm

 RF Shtorm or Project 23000 E Shtorm, is a Russian project to build a super carrier, to rival the Gerald R. Ford class carrier of United States Navy.

Class: Shtorm Class
Operators: Russian Navy

Aviation Facilities
Angled flight deck, Ski Jump, 2 EMALS, One set of arrestor gear.

The carrier will be equipped with both, Electromagnetic Aircraft Launching System(EMALS) as well as a ski jump, and will be able to launch next generation Russian jets like Sukhoi SU-75 Chrezmate and Mikoyan Mig-41. Its displacement will be approximately 1,00,000 tons, roughly the same as the Gerald R. Ford class, will be nuclear powered and would reqire a crew of around 3000 men and 200 officers, it will able to carry 90 to 100 aircrafts and would be able to operate in the open seas for about 5 months. The carrier will provide Russia its first ever capability to perform operations beyond Barent Seas and Black Seas. Four carriers are planned to be completed by 2030 and the total cost of the programme is estimated to be 240.35 billion rubles.
